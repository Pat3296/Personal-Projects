Health at a Glance - OECD Data Visualisations

Interactive visualisations exploring OECD health and digital statistics

Explore health trends across OECD countries with our interactive visualisations. Our platform provides insights into telehealth consultations, digital infrastructure, and internet access in households, including remote areas of Australia.

This website is created by Pat and Brayden for COS30045 Data Visualisation. The data sources include OECD Health Statistics, OECD Digital Transformation reports, and Australian Institute of Health and Welfare.

Telehealth Consultations 2019–2024

Explore the trends for in person vs telehealth consultations across OECD countries between 2019 and 2024. Use the dropdown menu to select a specific country and view how telehealth usage has changed over time.

National Digital Readiness for Healthcare

Comparing internet accessibility across households to evaluate the foundation for telehealth adoption and equitable digital healthcare delivery.

Australian Telehealth Utilization by State

Geographical distribution of Medicare-subsidized telehealth services. Select a year and item code to see service volumes across Australian states.

*Note: Item 91891 (Level B) covers standard consultations, while 91801 and 91900 (Level C) cover longer consultations (20-39 minutes). Certain years or categories in this dataset may show no data or limited services due to changes in Medicare eligibility or reporting periods.

Telehealth Access in Remote Australia

Investigate how telehealth services reach rural and remote communities in Australia. This visualization displays the rate of telehealth services for referred medical specialist attendances per 100 people, categorized by remoteness area from 2019–20 to 2022–23.

*Note: Rates are provided for both Video and Teleconference methods. Major Cities often show higher teleconference usage, while regional areas rely heavily on video-based specialist consultations.

Health Data Sharing Intensity

Comparison of how national health data is shared with business, government, and international stakeholders across OECD countries.

Share of adults avoiding online platforms due to privacy concerns

Percentage of adults aged 18 and over who avoid using certain websites, apps, or social media because of privacy concerns, sorted by country, and age group.
