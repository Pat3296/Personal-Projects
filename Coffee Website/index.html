About Us

Roasting and brewing only the finest coffee beans in the heart of Melbourne, Victoria, Hot Grind Coffee Roasters sources only the highest quality beans from around the globe to create a flavourful, genuine and unforgettable experience with your next cup. Started in 2025, Hot Grind Coffee Roasters has quickly become a staple for specialty coffee. Our team is made up of a group of coffee aficionados all coming from an extensive specialty coffee background, including roasters and brewers alike.

We source our specialty coffee beans across Central America, Asia and Africa and brew them right here in Melbourne, so you know they are freshly roasted and ready to enjoy at home. If you would prefer to have an in-house experience, our on-site cafe caters to those who appreciate specialty coffee and are looking for an ambient environment to enjoy their next brew in.

Our cafe is open to the public from Monday - Thursday (10am - 7pm) and hosts a variety of available coffees on a rotating basis, along with some sweet baked goods from Neighbourhood Bakeries to complement your cup.

We're looking forward to introducing HGCR into your daily grind!
